Our philosophy

01

Long-Term Orientation

We invest with a multi-year horizon, looking past short-term volatility to focus on the intrinsic value of businesses and the durability of income streams.

02

Disciplined Allocation

Capital is deployed deliberately across equities and bonds, with each position sized to reflect our conviction and the margin of safety available at the time of purchase.

03

Preservation First

Protecting capital is the foundation of compounding. We prioritise avoiding permanent loss of capital above chasing short-term returns or market trends.
